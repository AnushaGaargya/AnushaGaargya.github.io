About me

I, Anusha Gaargya, am an amateur artist, living in Karnataka, India. I have a degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering. But, ART is my passion.

I draw portraits and I try to paint out my thoughts. Because, I think I say better through pictures than words. I'm also interested in Indian classical music and I practice the same. I love playing the keyboard in my free time.

When I'm not drawing or listening to music, you'll find me reading Carl Sagan's quotes or looking up at the sky watching the birds fly. I have a thing for stars and galaxies.

Anu is an engineer by profession but an artist at heart. Few things are more joyous and liberating in life than discovering a channel for self-expression, and to cultivate and cherish that channel once found. Anu realized early in life that her channel was going to be art – painting and sketching, to be specific. From her first baby steps in this art form many moons ago to this day, she has been honing her craft sketch by sketch, continuing her quest to view the world through the mind’s eye and to communicate it through art. Her sketches and paintings clearly indicate that her ability as an artist has grown by leaps and bounds over the years, which is a testament to her perseverance and her passion to seek the truth and the essence of her unique existence through her craft. There is little doubt that great things await her in this journey of hers in the coming years.
